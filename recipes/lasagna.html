Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a class Italian dish renowned for its layers of rich flabors and comforting textures.

This dish is not only delicious but also customizable to suit various dietary preferences.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marina sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Cook ingredients and preheat oven
  2. Layer noodes, sauce and cheese
  3. Bake covered and serve warm!
